Probabilistic risk modeling to support decision-making under uncertainty in safety-critical systems.

## Overview

This repository explores how low-probability, high-impact risks can be incorporated into engineering decisions.

## Methodology

- Estimate event likelihood
- Quantify consequences
- Evaluate impact on system design

## Case Study: Space Radiation Risk Across Mission Profiles

This study compares radiation risk across different mission environments, highlighting how exposure and risk profiles change with altitude and shielding conditions.

### Scenarios

1. High-Altitude Flight  
   Examines radiation exposure in aviation environments with minimal shielding.

2. Low Earth Orbit (LEO)  
   Evaluates radiation risk within Earth's magnetosphere, considering shielding and orbital parameters.

3. Lunar Surface Mission  
   Assesses radiation exposure beyond Earth's protection, where deep space radiation becomes dominant.
